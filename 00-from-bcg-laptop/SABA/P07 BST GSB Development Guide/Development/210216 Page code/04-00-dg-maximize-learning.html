Getting the most from check-in conversations
  Check in
How are you finding the work so far? How are you progressing?

What are the key lessons you have learned so far?
  How has learning been applied?
Have you had an opportunity to apply the key lessons on this topic?

If so, what went well? What was challenging?
  How will learning be applied?
How can you apply what you've learned in future situations?

What can you do to actively seek opportunities to showcase what you've learned? What help do you need?

What are you excited about? Concerned about?
  Support
How can I help ensure that you continue to develop in this area? (E.g., role play, practice, facilitating a shadowing experience, etc.?)

Getting the most from LAB modules
Reflect Read the course descriptionand consider
  • What would you like to learn about this topic?
  • Do you see potentialvalue in what’s covered in this module?
  • What might you do if you learn something valuable?
Learn As new information is presented
  • Consider what itmeans to you in yourdaily work
  • How does it relate toyour role, your challenges, your goals?
  • Ask others if you are unsure
  • Be active in your learning
  • Take advantage of videos, downloads, quizzes, etc.
Act Make an action plan with oneor two concrete actions you can take!
  • What can you dotoday and next weekto practice whatyou’ve learned?
  • Consider talking to your manager, a mentor or a peer to share your plan (and celebrate success!)
  • How will you remind yourself to stick to your plan? Again, consider involving your manager, a mentor or a peer
