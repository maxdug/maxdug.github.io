Knowledge Navigator is a BCG internal Knowledge Database and a primary entry point for Learning & Development.

Framing the learner problem to solve?

  • Learners need to find (the right) content as quickly as possible...
  • Search is a recurring pain point indicated by users in key feedback channels

How big of a problem is this today?

  • 2/3 of monthly Navigator visits include at least on search
  • Monthly average¹ search volume: 507k
  • We know that:
    • Learners seem to use limited data on a search result to make a decision
    • We wan to remove friction wherever possible in accessing content

1. Average for time period January through June 2021

Goal

  • Analyze the existing state of how learning content and resources are displayed in Knowledge Navigator
  • Identify possible friction points/opportunities
  • Develop a roadmap for enhancements based on solid research

What is our hypthesis on impact?
