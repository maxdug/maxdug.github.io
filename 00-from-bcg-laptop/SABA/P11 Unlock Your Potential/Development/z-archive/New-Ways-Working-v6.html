NextGen Ways of Working
We live in a time of rapid change in which BCG and our clients are each on a journey towards the new digital reality. It is important to adopt NextGen Ways of Working and become digitally fluent – both internally and in our client work. This customized journey offers essential content around multi-disciplinary teaming, agile, and digital tooling.

NextGen WoW combines all of the above and especially puts a strong focus on value and impact, removing all non value-add work, in order to deliver work faster and in a more sustainable way. Training content on "Focus on outcome, not output" and Full transparency across BCG team and client will be added shortly.
