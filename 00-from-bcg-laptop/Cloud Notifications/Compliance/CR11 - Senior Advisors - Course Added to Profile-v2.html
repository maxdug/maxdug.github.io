Here at BCG, we are all online in some capacity and, as part of the BCG community, we are all expected to uphold BCG's values and act responsibly online.

Hence, as a member of the BCG community, we strongly encourage you to complete the Online@BCG training module. This course will familiarize you with BCG's social and digital communications policy, BCG's values, and educate you on how to conduct yourself online while working at BCG. This module will also help you navigate the grey areas of being online by providing you with useful information and testing your knowledge on the subject.

Click here to access the training module assigned to you on BCG's learning platform - LAB.

 

What you need to know:

  • The course will take approximately 30 minutes to complete
  • Submit the electronic acknowledgement at the end of each course to ensure a successful completion
  • If you do not have LAB access, please utilize the manual training material using the link below:
    • Online@BCG Manual Training
    • Once you've reviewed the training materials, please download and sign the acknowledgement form that is embedded within the training material and email it to compliancetraining@bcg.com to record a successful completion
