Value Creation & Impact Translate the output of analyses into recommendations
On-The-Job
  • Translate the output of analyses into plausible, implementable, and practical recommendations
  • Always think of the ‘So what’
  • Provide practical recommendations and your opinion based on the results
  • Be able to see the forest for the trees
  • Perform a "what if" analysis to check the practicality of your recommendations
  • Detail what needs to be done to ensure the feasibility of recommendations
  • Try to be clear, concise, and to the point in your communication
  • Before communicating to your stakeholders, think about the potential questions that they may have and try to address them beforehand
Coaching, Mentoring, & Feedback
  • Find a role model, e.g. someone how really creates impact in your eyes; observe and reflect what this person is doing and how; think of the behaviors you can learn from that
