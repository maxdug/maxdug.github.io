At the Side of The Road

Medium | Serigraphy / Screenprint

The hitchhiking armadillo represents men of the southern U.S. in this screenprint. I put him in the robes of a monk to establish the now rare, stoic nature I have met in the Men I have met. For this print, I utilized half-tones to help mix a background from photography with my illustration of the main character.
