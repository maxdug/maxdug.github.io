Downloading the mobile app

Have an upcoming flight, or a long cab ride? With the Saba Cloud app you can have the power of LAB at your fingertips. These following steps will walk you through the process of downloading and logging into the app - 60 seconds that will definitely be worth it.

As much as we live on our laptops we aren't always in an area where it's appropriate to open up a laptop to listen to a podcast, watch the latest LAB Talk, or dive into a lesson on the healthcare payer system. Enter the LAB mobile app - brining the power of BCG's learning management system to your phone where you can watch on the go, and download for offline view.

Step 1: Download the Saba Cloud app from the BCG Apps store.

Step 2: Open the app, type in bcg.sabacloud.com and then tap Enter

Step 3: Log into Okta

Use your BCG account information to log in, which is your BCG e-mail and password (this is the same password you use to log into Windows on your laptop).

Step 4: Verify your device

Tapping Send Code will send a verification code to your device which you then will need to enter and tap Verify.

Note: You’ll see I tapped ‘Do not challenge…’ as since I’ll be regularly coming back to this LAB app and don’t want to go through this process each time.

Step 5: Great success!

Now, go search for and download a few lessons for your upcoming flight, your workout, or while you put your feet up
