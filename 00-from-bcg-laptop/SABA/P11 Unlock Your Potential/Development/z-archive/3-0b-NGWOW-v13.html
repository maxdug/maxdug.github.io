Next Generation Ways of Working
A changing work environment bring new approaches to teaming and needs for seamless multidisciplinary collaboration. Next Generation Ways of Working prepare teams to thrive in this new environment with laser focus on value, integrated and sustainable teaming, united rhythms and approaches, and powerful tooling. This curated and modular learning journey contains what you need to know about Next Generation Ways of Working. To help navigate the extensive menu of training options below, we labeled it along the 7 dimensions of teaming tools & techniques. These elements are intended to be flexible to various team environments, but should be used as a framework to anchor new ways of working.

We recommend to begin with the fitness check to understand your starting point. For more resources, practical tips, and starter guides, visit our NextGen microsite.
