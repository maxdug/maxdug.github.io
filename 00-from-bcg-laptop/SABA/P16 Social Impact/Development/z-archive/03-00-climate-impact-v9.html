Climate
The Climate learning journey is a targeted deep dive into the data, tools, and thought leadership that drive our efforts to protect our planet and help our clients achieve sustainable competitive advantage.

Climate change is not a future threat. Launch this targeted SI learning journey to explore the latest in climate science and go deep on the most impactful levers for combatting climate change, including energy transitions, addressing high emitting sectors, new technologies like hydrogen, nature-based solutions and more.

Where to start your Climate learning journey? Dive in at the level that reflects your learning goals:
  • Level 0: Do a quick exploration of climate science and the elementary factbase
  • Level 1: Learn about BCG's climate commitments & actions to date
  • Level 2: Discover foundational thinking on the levers we can pull to most impactfully drive positive change
  • Level 3: Go deeper on each of these levers, including what's next in our fight against climate change
