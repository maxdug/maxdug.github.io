Career Ownership
On-The-Job
  • Be aware of your career goals and how those impact the work you do
  • Understand the opportunities you have and create avenues to test new skills and take on stretch projects
  • Build a growth mindset and identify skills that would be beneficial in your role
  • Take time to develop your professional network, both inside and outside of BCG
  • Managing upwards is a critical aspect of career ownership, develop strategies, and techniques to drive consensus and create lasting change
  • Be mindful of your work-life balance. Growth mindset, physical health, resilience, and wellness all directly impact the quality of your career and give you the confidence and courage to act
Coaching, Mentoring, & Feedback
  • Take control of your development, proactively identify stretch assignments, and create your own development plans, share widely with your manager and peers
  • If there are coaching or mentorship programs available to you, take advantage of them!
  • GSB coaching offering: gsbpeople.bcg.com/learning-development/one-on-one-coaching
  • GSB Manager Mentoring program: reach out to Rachael Arena
  • KT Mentoring program: gsbpeople.bcg.com/learning-development/mentoring
  • Look for mentors, both inside and outside of BCG
