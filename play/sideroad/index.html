At the Side of The Road

Medium | Serigraphy / Screenprint

This screenprint is a fun exploration of mixing illustration with half-toned photography. I enjoyed the juxtaposition of a transcendent hitchhiking armadillo “at the side of the road” where they are normally seen as road kill. (Also, I spent a lot of time eating at Waffle House around the time I made this print)
