Creating a plan on LAB
Plans are effective ways to curate content and classes you’ll want to participate in or attend. There are two ways to add learning to your transcript.

They both start by clicking the hamburger menu in the top left and selecting “Me” from the drop down menu.

Next, click the grey “Actions” button and select Add Learning.
