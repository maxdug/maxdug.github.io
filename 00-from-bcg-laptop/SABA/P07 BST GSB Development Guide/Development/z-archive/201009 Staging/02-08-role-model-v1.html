Role Model
On-The-Job
  • Be familiar with BCG values (available on LAB)
  • Treat all others with respect: customers, stakeholders, and peers
  • Think about ways you can better integrate yourself into the team or your office
  • Reflect of how often you say “thank you” to others
  • Practice providing constructive feedback to others
  • Manage risk in daily work being aware of BCG´s compliance regulations and making a good treatment of confidential information
  • Be committed and act as a role model within BCG
Coaching, Mentoring, & Feedback
  • Ask for feedback on your behavior from others; actively listen to their feedback without justifying; act on their feedback
  • Talk openly to your Line Manager and ask for support
  • Proactively ask for support from your peers
