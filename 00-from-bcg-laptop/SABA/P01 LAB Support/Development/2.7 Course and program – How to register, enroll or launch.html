Course and program—How to register, enroll, or launch

Courses live within a program and it’s important to ensure you’ve added the content to your transcript correctly.

We recommend you bookmark related programs if you plan to complete the entire series.

From within a program, you are not required to complete all courses (unless the course is mandatory) and you can usually launch any course in any order you’d like.

For virtual or in-person courses, you may be given an option to select a specific class session to attend. You should first select the appropriate Path available and then select your preferred class session for each course before completing your registration.
