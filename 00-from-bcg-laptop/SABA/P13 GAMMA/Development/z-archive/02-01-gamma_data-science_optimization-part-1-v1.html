Optimization Training - Part 1
Virtual classroom instructions
In this virtual set-up:
  1. Watch a series of videos covering the theoretical topics. These videos must be watched sequentially.
  2. As you review the videos, if you have questions please write them down. Q&A and live sessions will provide an opportunity to share your questions.
  3. Connect on time to the live sessions for practice.
    • During the live sessions, Pascal Van Hentenryck, the professor, will provide instructions for completing the exercises.
    • The plenary session will remain open during the entire practice session.
    • You may request a 1 to 1 session with Pascal at any time to the session facilitator. Your training facilitator is reachable on the plenary session chat, or on Slack.
  4. During the 3-day training period, Pascal is available for questions via the Slack Channel advanced_optimization_virtual_training
