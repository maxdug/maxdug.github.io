Citi Mobile Banking

Agency | Fluid

Role | Senior Visual and UX Design

Citibank commissioned our team to develop the CitiGold App during an extended onsite collaboration with their fintech innovation group. I worked on the money transfer workstream, crafting user flows, refining UI components, and liaising with governance to clarify and refine designs.

Mapping Innovation

We worked in an agile environment with a small, nimble team focused on quick iteration. We continuously mapped new screens into user flows to describe each interaction to both governance and the larger app team.

Morning whiteboarding sessions aligned the team and facilitated innovation
User Flows allowed us to communicate with stakeholders like sharing a storyboard

Innovation Within Boundries

We aimed to simplify money transfers while adhering to banking laws and existing branding guidelines. We introduced features like our "slide-to-pay" button that enhanced customer confidence during transactions.
