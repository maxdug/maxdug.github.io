Astoria Runners

A few years ago I joined the local running group for the best neighborhood in NYC. They've since become an integral part of my life, and not being able to help myself, I created an updated identity that we've started to utilize. This page is in progress and will be updated soon.

Logo exploration

There was a mix of two existing logos used across the groups digital presence. I wanted to make a new mark while still paying homage to the original designs. The overarching theme was the runners a red sash.
