Just Keep Truckin

Medium | Photo-Documentary

“Just Keep Truckin” is a photo documentation of the daily life of truck drivers. Recognizing that truckers are a diverse group that make up a significant portion of the global workforce, I was interested in documenting their unique culture and work-life balance. These images were captured while the drivers were filling up at truck stops, stuck on the side of the interstate, or recuperating after a long journey. I am very thankful that the truckers I met were willing to share the details of their occupation with me. Despite my interruption to their daily lives and work, they spared their time to answer questions and participate in these photos. The original format of this work was a small book and gallery show. I also translated many of the photos into serigraphic prints.
