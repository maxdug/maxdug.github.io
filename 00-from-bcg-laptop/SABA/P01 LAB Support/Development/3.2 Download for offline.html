Download content for offline use

Getting on a flight? Download content to play while on the go.

First, register for your course
Open your Saba app, then search and register for the course you wish to download. Already registered? Tap the in-progress button on your main dashboard to find your course.

• Note: You are automatically registered for a course when you launch it on the desktop or mobile app

Download your course
Once your course downloads, you'll receive a notification from the app. Don’t see a download button even after registering? Some content is not available offline so you will need to either stream or watch on your desktop.

Access your content
Tap More in the lower right of your screen, Downloads, and then applicable content. Be aware that downloaded content is only available for 30 days. You can work offline and the course’s status will update once you are back online.
