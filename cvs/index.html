CVS
Reimagining Health & Wellness Online

Agency | Razorfish

Role | Associate Design Director

While at Razorfish, I led multiple projects for their long-term partnership with CVS. During this time, I developed a range of B2B and B2C experiences, helping to elevate their design system, streamline customer flows, and implement a new focus on digital accessibility.

Establishing a Culture of Collaboration

Starting at the kickoff of each CVS project, I would share the process and overarching strategy, so that stakeholders understand the how and why behind design decisions.

Customizing Deliverables for Client Needs

We efficiently collaborated with the client and accelerated the design process for the CVS Drug Information Center by integrating content analysis into the user flow.

Elevating Look & Feel

Our vision was to elevate their design from clinical to health and wellness using geometric shapes, icons, playful line work, and lifestyle photography.

Playful Iconography Throughout

We introduced "Sparks," a new icon system representing categories and concepts. The designed each icon to balance geometry, abstraction, and minimalism.

Leading With a System-First Approach

Designing from the atomic level up and emphasizing long-term modularity increased our prototyping speed and enabled accessibility reviews at the component level.

CVS Shop Redesign

CVS challenged us to reimagine e-commerce for health and wellness. One of the ways we achieved this was with a new fulfillment system that mimicked a model used by food delivery apps, giving customers the ability to set their fulfillment preference at any point in the journey.

CVS Drug Information Center

CVS provides drug information as part of their mission to be a go-to resource in health and wellness. We had a unique challenge: to support pharmacy customers and drive search engine traffic.

CVS Media Exchange® (CMX)

CMX leverages data from CVS’s distinctive customer base to provide personalized brand marketing. They came to us to relaunch CMX from an outdated single information page to a microsite. The unique challenge with this project was to mix business and data messaging with a design that shared CVS's health and wellness mission.
