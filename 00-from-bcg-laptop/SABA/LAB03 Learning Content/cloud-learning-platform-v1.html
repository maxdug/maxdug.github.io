Learning@BCG Cloud Learning Platform

Cloud Learning Vision

  • Provide a world-class learning experience for all BCG that differentiates our people, enables learners to build their capabilities, improve performance and increase confidence, leading to higher retention and satisfaction.
  • Improve learner efficiency, finding relevant learning content more rapidly, reduce time spent ramping up on new topics.
  • Provide cost beneficial and rapid ways to deliver learning to large groups, provide means to scale with growth and diversity of learners.

Answers to Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I see home pages for other groups?

A: The LAB home page is personalized by group. L&D team members can change their settings to select to view any of the six different personalization groups. Just remember to change it back so you can see what’s relevant for your default group. Click here for a brief tutorial.


Q: Where do I find the consulting Starter Kits?

A: Consulting Team Starter Kits are now called Launch Kits and can be found on LAB. Click here to view the First Year, Project Leader and Senior Hire Launch Kits.


Q: How do I link to content on the new LAB?

A: You can continue to link to content topics on LAB or deep link to a course. To learn how to get links to content in LAB Click here.
