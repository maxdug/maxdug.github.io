Ticketmaster for Artists

Agency | Razorfish within Publicis Groupe

Role | Asscosiate Design Director

Creative Team | Sonia Acosta, AJ Teiger

Ticketmaster needed help defining and launching their new B2B platform for music managers. This new platform harnessed the power of Ticketmaster’s unparalleled data. It provided powerful insights, making it easy for managers to make smarter decisions.

Visual Identity

For a design where the challenge was to be bold in a space where the client's client are rock stars, we intoducted a Spotlight metaphor for the way Artist Inc. experiences live events, that carried through into shapes, colors, and photography. We utilized a dark background to create foundation for the bold color contrast, adding excitement, warmth, and energy.

Homepage

We employed our spotlight motif for a simple hero image that highlights the headline. Across our design system, we came up with a card style reminiscent of a record sleeve, with cards alternating between our key colors. We iterated with both horizontal and vertical carousels and cards that can handle a spectrum of meta data quantity. In situations where the imagery needs to relay detail to the user, we pulled the image out of the circle and provided more real-estate.

TM1 App

We stacked the cards on the mobile version of the app's details page to ensure important information isn’t overlooked in a carousel. Playing again off the spotlight motif, we framed information and overlapped full images with thin outlined circles.

Resources

On the resources page we crafted a case study piece that focused on featured artists, using their logos as key identifiers. We followed complex modules like this, with simple iconography based cards to balance the page flow.

Chart Details

We used Ticketmaster charts to tease the power of the TM1 app and push users towards conversion. For these pages, we didn’t use a spotlight hero, opting to not break the flow of the horizontal lines. We made the complex table mobile-friendly by focusing on data and removing descriptive text.

Case Study

We made the desktop version of Case Study Details easier to read by using a narrower column width. When there is enough space horizontally, we added a sticky module with related case studies. For content, we created a image collage component where data points integrated by sharing an edge within an image frame.
