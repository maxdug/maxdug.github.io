Citi Mobile Banking

Agency | Fluid

Role | Senior Visual and UX Design

Citi tasked us to rev-invent their mobile app experience, during an on-site contract. I worked as Senior UX, working on the money transfer workstream through research and design.

Mapping Innovation

We worked in an agile environment with a small nimble team focused on quick iteration. We continually mapped user flows to help describe each interaction to a governance group.

Challenges & Solutions

Our goal was to streamline the transfer of money for the customers, while keeping within the boundaries of banking laws and the existing Citi look and feel. We innovated with new interactions like “slide-to-pay” that gave customers increased validation when completing a transaction.
