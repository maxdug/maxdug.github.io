WELCOME TO BCG

Your first few days at BCG are unique and exciting, and probably overwhelming as well. There are many things to learn and people to meet, and the pressure of performing quickly can be daunting. Where can you find some good tips to help you jumpstart your BCG career? What are the common pitfalls and how do other senior hires cope with such challenges?

The Senior Hire Launch Kit is here to help. This self-paced online curriculum available on LAB will quickly equip you with the essential skills to thrive at BCG. You can choose from a range of topics that are most relevant and revisit when you need them. You are not expected to complete all the modules in your first week.

To understand more about BCG, check out the BCG's History, DNA, and Value primer in Intro to BCG. If you expect to lead a project soon, review the Project Management topic to understand BCG's approach. If you are going to participate in a proposal pitch, check out the Proposal Excellence topic. In addition, you can also reach out to the Proposal Excellence Team if you need further support.
