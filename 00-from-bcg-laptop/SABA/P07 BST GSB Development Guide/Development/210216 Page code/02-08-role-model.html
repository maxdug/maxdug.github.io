  • Reflect of how often you say “thank you” to others
  • Practice providing constructive feedback to others
  • Manage risk in daily work being aware of BCG´s compliance regulations and making a good treatment of confidential information

Coaching, Mentoring, & Feedback

  • Talk openly to your Line Manager and ask for support
  • Proactively ask for support from your peers
