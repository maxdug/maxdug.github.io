FAQ
Can I self-register for trainings? Some trainings have open registration policies, some do not. When clicking the “enroll” button, you will receive either a registration confirmation email or a pending approval email. For those classes requiring approval, once the approver has registered/refused you for the training, you will get an automatic notification. Tip: if there is a large capacity/no limit to a training, you may expect an open registration policy. If seats are limited, this training will likely have an approval process.
 
Can I find all trainings available to me on the newly enhanced LAB platform? We intend to host all classes on the LAB platform, but not until at least end of 2021.
 Tip: if you are unsure about the existence of a training, feel free to reach out to one of our L&D contacts.
 
I have a question about a training I am enrolled in or would like to enroll in, how do I proceed? On the class page for classes you have registered or look to register, the training coordinator name is visible, feel free to reach out!
 
I want to cancel my participation for a training, how to proceed? If there is a “drop registration” button available, we recommend you use that functionality. Tip: if the class has cancellation fee or a deadline to drop the training, you should contact the class training coordinator to discuss your cancellation.
 
As BCGer member of Gamma, am I eligible for any Gamma technical training? Some trainings have limited capacity and high demand, eligibility conditions may apply such as tenure, area of focus, application of knowledge, etc.
 
How do I locate self-paced learning on LAB? LAB provides access to many self-paced courses, podcasts, recordings, videos, etc. This includes access to LinkedIn Learning courses. To locate self-paced trainings, go to the LAB Home page and start to explore using the Browse feature or Learning Catalog.
 
How do I request certifications (AWS, Azure etc.)? Reach out to your L&D contact to explore the many opportunities within the BCG network.
 
How do I request approval / reimbursement for external training opportunities? Please use the survey link available on the Confluence page: https://confluence.gamma.bcg.com/display/WSLAG/Online+recommended+trainings
 
Generally speaking, I have a question about L&D, who should I reach out to? You may reach out to your L&D contact (Emeline Allard for C&X and Katie Raymond for A&S) or your CD manager. Tip: you can find all the learning contacts on the landing page of the platform.
