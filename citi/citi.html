Citi Mobile Banking

Agency | Fluid

Role | Senior Visual and UX Design

Citi Bank tasked us to rev-invent their mobile app experience, during an on-site contract. I worked as Senior UX Designer on the money transfer workstream.

Mapping Innovation

We worked in an agile environment with a small nimble team focused on quick iteration. We continually mapped user flows to help describe each interaction to a governance group.

Morning whiteboarding sessions aligned the team and facilitated innovation
User Flows allowed us to communicate with stakeholders like sharing a storyboard

Innovation Within Boundries

Our goal was to streamline money transfer, within the boundaries of banking laws and the existing branding guidelines. We innovated with interactions like a “slide-to-pay” button that increased customer confidence when completing transactions.
