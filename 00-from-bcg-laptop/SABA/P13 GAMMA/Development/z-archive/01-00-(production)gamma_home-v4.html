FAQ
Can I self-register for trainings?

Some trainings have open registration policies, some do not. When clicking the “enroll” button, you will receive either a registration confirmation email or a pending approval email. For those classes requiring approval, once the approver has registered/refused you for the training, you will get an automatic notification.

Tip: if there is a large capacity/no limit to a training, you may expect an open registration policy. If seats are limited, this training will likely have an approval process.

Can I find all trainings available to me on this site?

The SABA platform is our brand-new L&D platform, we intend to host all classes there but not until at least end of 2021.

Tip: if you are unsure about the existence of a training, feel free to reach our to your L&D training coordinator.

I have a question about a training I am enrolled in or would like to enroll in, how do I proceed?

On the class page for classes you have registered or look to register, the training coordinator name is visible, feel free to reach out!

I want to cancel my participation for a training, how to proceed?

If there is a “drop registration” button available, we recommend you use that functionality.

Tip: if the class has cancellation fee or a deadline to drop the training, you should contact your training coordinator to discuss your cancellation.

As BCGer member of Gamma, am I eligible for any Gamma technical training?

Some trainings have limited capacity and high demand, eligibility conditions may apply such as tenure, area of focus, application of knowledge, etc.

How do I find self-paced trainings on this site?

The intent is to host all our L&D material on this platform, you will be able to find live (both virtual and on-site), blended (self-paced + live) as well as e-learning opportunities.

How do I request certifications (AWS, Azure etc.)?

Reach out to your L&D contact to explore the many opportunities within the BCG network.

How do I request approval / reimbursement for external training opportunities?

Please use the survey link available on the Confluence page: https://confluence.gamma.bcg.com/display/WSLAG/Online+recommended+trainings

Generally speaking, I have a question about L&D, who should I reach out to?

You may reach out to your CD manager or to your L&D contact (Katie Raymond for A&S, Emeline Allard for C&X)

Tip: you can find all the learning contacts on the landing page of the platform.
