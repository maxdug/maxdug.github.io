Aspen Institute

The Aspen Institute for Leadership is an emerging partnership that we have. There are 2 different kinds of programs available:

Socrates Program

The Socrates program provides a forum for emerging leaders from various professions to convene and explore contemporary issues through expert-moderated dialogue.

Executive Leadership Seminars

The Executive Leadership Seminars on leadership, values, and good society challenge participants to achieve meaningful leadership growth through a critical examination of their core values.

For more information please talk to Licia Minervini or Chadia de Simone.

NAMR Leadership Coaching

Through BCG’s Leadership Coaching offering, executive coaches work with our most senior client leaders in the context of broader BCG initiatives to help them navigate change, adopt new ways of working, and achieve their highest priority business outcomes. Our differentiated coaching cadre is comprised of former C-suite and other senior executives, all of whom have led their own organizations through transformational change.

Our coaching team works closely with the core BCG case team, creating feedback loops that allow for quick issue identification and resolution. This integration also provides our coaches with deep context around the organization’s strategic imperatives and business priorities.

Want to learn more? Use Navigator to search for updated material or directly contact Jen Thomas, Founder and Director of Leadership Coaching.
