FAQs
See All FAQs    See Glossary
What can I search for on LAB?
Search LAB for on-demand lessons, playlists, and recordings of past LAB Talks.
What is the difference between a chapter and lesson?
Chapters are group of sequential lessons that complete specific learning goals.
When should I attempt the Assessment?
Anytime! Reccomended for those with basic analytics skills, but can be used to identify future learning. Take as many times as you like.
What is a LAB Talk?
A Live LAB Talk, formerly known as Virtual Training is a recording from BCG experts.
How can get access to LAB material for internal use?
Email learning@bcg.com and include title of the learning content you want to access.
How do I find live programs for my role?
Click Live Courses from the LAB homepage anf filter future programs offered to your role, your office and the type of program.
