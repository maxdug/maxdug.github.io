Social Impact Foundations
At BCG, we work with our clients to transform their core business models, capabilities, and operations to create positive economic, social, and environmental impact in ways that are profitable for the long term.

The Social Impact Foundations learning journey was designed to help you become familiar with the concepts, thinking, and actions that underpin our collective impact. This journey is for all BCGers who want to explore the building blocks of our SI practice and deepen your understanding of the tools and partnerships BCG is developing to drive change around the world.

Where to start your SI Foundations learning journey? Dive in at the level that reflects your learning goals:
  • Level 0: Explore the building blocks of SI, as well as our practice area's purpose and framework for impact
  • Level 1: Get a snapshot of BCG's broader SI commitments
  • Level 2: Go deeper on the business imperative of social change, and how business and capital can be forces for good

Check out the case studies across Levels 1 & 2 for examples of how our work with social, public, and private sector clients contributes to a more sustainable and inclusive future.
