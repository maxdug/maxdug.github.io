Ticketmaster for Artists

Agency | Razorfish within Publicis Groupe

Role | Asscosiate Design Director

Creative Team | Sonia Acosta, AJ Teiger

Ticketmaster needed help defining and launching their new B2B platform for music managers. This new platform harnessed the power of Ticketmaster’s unparalleled data. It provided powerful insights, making it easy for managers to make smarter decisions.

Visual Identity

For a design where the challenge was to be bold in a space where the client's client are rock stars, we intoducted a Spotlight metaphor for the way Artist Inc. experiences live events, that carried through into shapes, colors, and photography. We utilized a dark background to create foundation for the bold color contrast, adding excitement, warmth, and energy.
